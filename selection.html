What Are Subdivisions?
Subdivisions are certain groups of a force that specialise in certain areas. For example, SWAT specialises in tactics and weapons while Air-One focuses mostly on Air Support and the list goes on.
Subdivisions are run by certain people of the community. To enter a subdivision, you must attend a training associated with the division.
This page will assist you with certain divisions. Most of the tools are timers etc and shall help you when on the field. More are being added as time goes on! The navigation bar is located at the top right hand side of this page.
